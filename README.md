<h2 id="step-1-understand-why-you-were-banned-">Step 1: Understand Why You Were Banned ❓</h2>
<p>Check your email, Xbox notifications, or Microsoft account messages to see why they pulled the ban hammer. Typical ban triggers include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Using unauthorized software or cheating tools  </li>
<li>Engaging in toxic or abusive behavior  </li>
<li>Sharing accounts or suspicious account activity  </li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Pro tip:</strong> Familiarize yourself with Microsoft’s Terms of Use before crafting your appeal.</p>
<hr>
<h2 id="step-2-collect-your-information-">Step 2: Collect Your Information 📝</h2>
<p>Before reaching out, make sure you have:</p>
<ul>
<li>Your Microsoft account email address  </li>
<li>Your Xbox Gamertag or subscription ID  </li>
<li>The exact date your ban or suspension kicked in  </li>
<li>Any error codes, screenshots, or messages you received  </li>
</ul>
